STANDINGS

See where you stand among all the registered CAs! Most efficient CAs will receive prizes from Alcheringa every three weeks. Bonus points will be awarded to CAs for their exceptional work.

POINTS SYSTEM

Each Facebook share equals 15 Points

Each Idea equals 50 Points

Each Venue equals 300 Points

Each POC equals 50 Points

Each Referral equals 50 Points

After every three weeks, our tri-weekly winner will be announced and all your points will be reset to zero. For the next three weeks, every Campus Ambassador will again start from zero and battle their way up to the top of the leaderboard. We will be glad if you become the next tri-weekly winner of our Campus Ambassador Programme.

Each of your input (FB Share, Idea, Venue, POC, Referral) will be reviewed by our team. Upon scrutiny, if certain input is found to be not matching our standards, respective points will be adjusted. FB share should have visibility set to 'public' to be eligible for points. Repeated sharing of the same post will not amount to addition of points. Each Idea, Venue, POC submitted should be genuine. Campus Ambassadors are requested not to follow unfair means. Continuous violation of rules will lead to debarment from the Campus Ambassador Programme.
